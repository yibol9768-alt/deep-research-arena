What v4c does. Same 11 pillars as v4b, same weight vector, same multiplicative reach gate. The change is in how each pillar value enters the composite: instead of raw p_i ∈ [0, 1], each measurement is standardised to z_i = (p_i − μ_i) / σ_i (population mean/std across the whole sample) and squashed back into [0, 1] via a mild sigmoid. Saturated pillars (low σ across agents) collapse toward 0.5 for everyone — they stop contributing. Wide-spread pillars (source_diversity, factual_exactness, checklist) automatically take the discriminating load. This is the same principle behind financial composite indices and multi-criteria scoring: budget each input's contribution by its actual information content, not its nominal weight.

Reading the v4 verdict

The headline question this view answers is whether v4 actually rewrites the verdict on existing reports. Three takeaways from the 25-run sample:

  1. Head and tail are the same in v4 and v2. camel-ai is #1 in both; langchain-odr and gpt-researcher hold the tail in both. v4 is not in opposition to v2 ── it builds on it.
  2. The middle of the table reshuffles. deerflow jumps +2 ranks (v2 #6 → v4 #4) and storm jumps an effective +4 (deep v2 tail when ranked there; v4 #5). The shared cause is that v4 rewards high factual_exactness and perfect internal_consistency even when v2's url_coverage alone underweighted them.
  3. v4 confirms the fabricated-URL critique. gpt-researcher and langchain-odr both score factual_exactness = 0.00 on every sampled report ── their URLs may be reachable in places, but the atomic facts cited from those URLs are not corroborated by the pages. This is the "fluent fabrication" mode v2 catches via reach, and v4 catches via a second, independent signal.

The headline ranking still uses v2 in production because the truth-gate inversion finding (/how-it-works) is the primary methodological contribution and is cleanest at the v2 4-input scale. v4 is the next layer of the same idea ── more dimensions, same multiplicative gate, more robust verdict on the agents that v2 marks as borderline.
