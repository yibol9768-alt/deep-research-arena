## Editorial Decision Letter

**Decision: MAJOR REVISION (lean Reject in current form)**

The paper proposes a Docker-containerized three-source benchmark with deterministic citation ground truth and a "fluent hallucinator" empirical demonstration that text-quality rubrics miss URL-fabrication failure modes. The conceptual contribution is genuine D&B-track material in a real, currently-unoccupied niche between WebArena (interactive) and BrowseComp-Plus (reproducible-static). The empirical headline (V1↔V3 ranking inversion driven by GPT-Researcher's 97.5% fabrication) is timely given the 2025-2026 hallucinated-citations discourse.

However, the manuscript as submitted has four blocking issues that the synthesizer cannot soften:

**(C1) Scope mismatch.** Abstract / §1 / §3.2 / Tab.2 advertise "100 tasks." All empirical results (Tab.3 leaderboard, Tab.4 per-dimension, Tab.5 ablation, Fig.2 fluent-hallucinator) are computed on a 30/31-task common subset with the remaining 70 tasks "in progress." For a D&B-track paper, this is missing data, not a writing fix. All five reviewers flagged this independently.

**(C2) Asymmetric n on the rank-1 agent.** Tab.4 documents CAMEL-AI at n=56 while every competitor remains at n=30. The 26-task extension is described as a "stability check" without explanation of why the extension was selectively applied to the leader. Inside a Bradley-Terry leaderboard, asymmetric sampling on the top-ranked entry is a methodology bug, not a presentation issue.

**(C3) Same-family judge.** Both the agent backbone and the LLM judge are DeepSeek V4 flash. The textbook self-enhancement-bias setup (cited as [llmjudge2024]) is acknowledged for "single-judge" weakness only — same-family bias is not addressed even at the level of a robustness slice.

**(C4) Scoring-formula mismatch (synthesizer-discovered).** Eq.1 specifies a 9-dimension V3 composite (url_coverage, reachability gate, quote_match, judge, citation_alignment, analysis_depth, spec_compliance, presentation, with weights 0.20/gate/0.20/0.20/0.15/0.10/0.10/0.05). However, all 156 local score files use only 6 dimensions (url_coverage, url_reachability, quote_match, claim_nli, markdown_spec, checklist) and the actual leaderboard composite is `reachability × (0.40·url_cov + 0.40·judge + 0.20·spec)` — a V2 formula that pre-dates Eq.1's V3 specification. **Either Eq.1 must be rewritten to match what was actually computed, or the V3 verifiers (citation_alignment, analysis_depth, presentation) must be run on the existing report .md files and the leaderboard recomputed.** Submitting a paper whose stated scoring formula does not match its evaluated scoring is not acceptable.

In addition, 14 Major and 6 Minor issues require addressing (full list below). The D&B track explicitly weighs reproducibility, scope honesty, and statistical rigor — none of which the current submission satisfies. Recommended pathway:

1. Resolve the scoring-formula discrepancy (C4) — either rewrite Eq.1 + §3.4 + Tab.4 to V2 formulation, or run the V3 verifiers on existing reports.
2. Run the remaining 70 tasks × 9 agents (or 70 tasks × surviving non-broken agents) through the chosen scoring formula.
3. Run a non-DeepSeek judge on at least the top-tier vs. GPT-Researcher contrast (e.g., 30 tasks × 4 agents × GPT-5-chat = 120 judge calls).
4. Add an explicit §Reproducibility with image digests, seed/decoding params, judge model versioning, and code/data release plan.
5. Disclose the NLI dimension's dropping history.
6. Split the Tab.1 "Det. GT" column into citation-GT vs. task-completion-GT, fix WebArena's column.
7. Add the citation-faithfulness literature.
8. Run a URL-stuffing null-agent attack and report whether the gate catches it.
9. Run a 0.1-gate-floor sensitivity sweep.
10. Deliver the intent-stratified analysis the paper advertises as a contribution.

If these are addressed in good faith, the paper has a clear path to Accept at a future venue (NeurIPS 2026 D&B with ≥6 weeks to camera-ready, or ICLR 2026 with full rebuttal cycle). As submitted, recommend Reject; with revision it becomes a competitive 7-8/10 D&B paper.
