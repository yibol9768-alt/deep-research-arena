## Notes

- **Golden oracles are intentionally empty.** `golden.triples_path` points to `data/golden/dr_cross_v3_<ID>.json` but no such files are committed yet, and the author of this expansion assumes fact_kg scoring is down-weighted for the first pass of these tasks. Coverage-checklist entries likewise are not yet added beyond 0001-0007; add new keys to `checklists_v3.json` as they are authored.
- **No product names or Reddit post titles are hardcoded** in the intents — every task leaves concrete picks to the agent, so the oracle is never leaked by the prompt.
- **Placeholders** `__SHOPPING__`, `__REDDIT__`, `__WIKIPEDIA__`, `__GITLAB__`, `__SHOPPING_ADMIN__` are used in `start_url` and `citation_policy.must_be_in_domain` to match the 0001-0007 convention; resolve them at load time to the actual sandbox hosts.
- **`markdown_spec` / `citation_policy` / `expected_steps`** are tuned per-difficulty: easy tasks aim for 800-word, 8-citation briefs with ~10-12 steps; medium tasks aim for 1000 words, 10 citations, 16-18 steps; hard 3-site tasks require 1100-1200 words, 12 citations, and 22-24 steps.
