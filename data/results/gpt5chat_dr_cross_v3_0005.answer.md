## Buying Priority Ranking

If budget tightens below target, prioritize in the following order:

1. **Chair (High Priority)** – Core comfort/ergonomics for daily stability.
2. **Desk** – Secure surface sized for room constraints.
3. **Lighting** – Maintain visual and mental ease.
4. **Monitor Stand** – Ergonomic upgrade to be delayed if resources thin; books can substitute.
5. **Cleaner Kit and Organizer** – Final additions for maintenance efficiency.

Avoid supersized gaming desks or high-decor lamps until strong income continuity. Reddit’s *PersonalFinance crowd wisdom* advises saving that surplus toward emergency funds rather than impulse décor.
