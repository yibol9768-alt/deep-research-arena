## Volume vs engagement winners

By raw submission count, **Tech regulation** dominates the front pages of /f/technology — it's what people are *posting* about. By average comments per submission, however, **Privacy / security** drives the deepest discussion. These differ — a classic case of a topic that is over-represented in posts but under-represented in actual discussion (or vice versa).

## Methodology and caveats

- Theme classification is keyword-regex; a title can be assigned to multiple themes if multiple keyword groups hit. This slightly inflates raw counts but keeps the analysis stable for ambiguous titles like 'AI chip cuts at Nvidia'.
- Two pages of submissions (~50 posts) is a thin sample; broader trends would benefit from a 3-month rolling pull.
- We ignore body text — themes derive from titles only.
