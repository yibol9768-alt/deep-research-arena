**1. "World-Class ANC" on Srhythm NC35 ($21.93)**
- [Product](http://localhost:7770/srhythm-nc35-noise-cancelling-headphones-real-over-ear-wireless-lightweight-durable-foldable-bluetooth-headset-bundles-with-s7-true-wireless-earphones-with-bluetooth-5-0-world-class-anc-earbuds.html): Claims "World-Class ANC Earbuds"
- [Wikipedia](http://localhost:8090/content/wikipedia_en_all_nopic/Noise-cancelling_headphones): ANC requires microphones, amplifiers, and phase-cancellation circuitry — it is power-hungry and requires proper engineering. The [Active noise control](http://localhost:8090/content/wikipedia_en_all_nopic/Active_noise_control) article notes that effective ANC requires real-time waveform analysis. A $21.93 product bundling over-ear headphones AND true wireless earbuds is **inconsistent** with the cost of implementing genuine ANC circuitry. This is likely passive sound isolation, not active noise cancellation.

**2. "Built-in Loudspeaker" on Ulian MH5 ($19.99)**
- [Product](http://localhost:7770/ulian-over-ear-headphones-wireless-bluetooth-headphones-loudspeaker-over-ear-headset-noise-cancelling-headphones-40-hours-playtime-3-modes-for-travel-work-mh5-black.html): Claims to include both a "Loudspeaker" AND "Noise Cancelling" AND "40 Hours Playtime"

Running ANC, Bluetooth, AND a built-in speaker simultaneously would drain a [lithium-ion battery](http://localhost:8090/content/wikipedia_en_all_nopic/Lithium-ion_battery) far faster than the claimed 40 hours.

**3. "Subwoofer" in NCRD Wireless Earbuds ($287.64)**
- [Product](http://localhost:7770/ncrd-wireless-earbuds-bluetooth-headphones-ear-hook-headphones-wireless-earbuds-subwoofer-earphones-noise-cancelling-lightweight-sweatproof-for-gym-running-workout-in-ear-headsets-color-gray.html): Claims "Subwoofer Earphones"

**4. "Noise Cancelling" on Bluetooth V4.1 Headset ($14.99)**

## KEY FINDINGS SUMMARY

**Price-Quality Correlation is Weak in the Budget Tier**: Products under $30 like the [Ulian MH5](http://localhost:7770/ulian-over-ear-headphones-wireless-bluetooth-headphones-loudspeaker-over-ear-headset-noise-cancelling-headphones-40-hours-playtime-3-modes-for-travel-work-mh5-black.html) (5.0/5) and [Asnrc TWS](http://localhost:7770/asnrc-wireless-earbuds-bluetooth-5-0-wireless-earbuds-sports-earbuds-noise-cancelling-wireless-ear-buds-tws-wireless-earbuds-audifonos-bluetooth-inalambricos-wireless-headphones-for-iphone-android.html) (4.7/5) achieve high ratings despite implausible feature clustering (ANC + loudspeaker + 40h battery for $19.99). Meanwhile, premium products like the [JBL Live 660NC](http://localhost:7770/jbl-live-660nc-wireless-over-ear-noise-cancelling-headphones-with-long-lasting-battery-and-voice-assistant-black.html) at $199.95 get only 2.8/5.

**Marketing Terminology Diverges Significantly from Technical Reality**: The term "noise cancelling" is used by both [genuine ANC products](http://localhost:7770/sony-wh-1000xm4-wireless-noise-canceling-over-ear-headphones-black-with-sony-wla-ns7-wireless-tv-adapter-bundle-2-items.html) at $406 and [budget headsets](http://localhost:7770/bluetooth-headset-wireless-bluetooth-earpiece-v4-1-bluetooth-earbuds-headphones-lightweight-in-ear-earphones-noise-cancelling-with-mic-and-handsfree-for-business-office-driving.html) at $14.99, despite Wikipedia-confirmed evidence that genuine ANC requires sophisticated phase-cancellation circuitry.

**Reddit Enthusiasts and Store Reviewers are Two Different Audiences**: Products rated 4.0+ on the store (like the [Skullcandy Crusher Evo](http://localhost:7770/skullcandy-crusher-evo-wireless-over-ear-headphone-chill-grey.html) at 4.2/5) get zero discussion among [/f/headphones](http://localhost:9999/f/headphones/126745) enthusiasts. Conversely, the [FIIO FT3](http://localhost:9999/f/headphones/126750) with its 350Ω impedance generates lively enthusiast discussion but has no store presence. This suggests two completely separate markets: casual consumers and audiophiles.
