## 7 Myths the Community Now Considers Debunked

### Myth 1: Cherry MX switches are the absolute best and most durable
- **Wiki**: [Cherry MX](http://localhost:8090/content/wikipedia_en_all_nopic/A/Cherry_MX) – Cherry MX are popular but Gateron/Kailh often match or exceed quality
- **Reddit**: [Switches](http://localhost:9999/submission_images/eecb181688ff552884085439d1cd960eef19b6e38ea0eec718c79088b1657b67.jpg) – Community discusses switch alternatives

### Myth 2: Hot-swapping damages keyboards and reduces reliability
- **Wiki**: [Hot swapping](http://localhost:8090/content/wikipedia_en_all_nopic/A/Hot_swapping) – Hot-swap is now standard and tested for durability
- **Reddit**: [First ever Keyboard Build :)](http://localhost:9999/submission_images/d12711580bbe524104bb215ae9e7a1f0f27dda331bccbc1d6df5567fd8f68eba.jpg) – New builders successfully use hot-swap

### Myth 3: Optical switches are just a gimmick
- **Wiki**: [Optical switch](http://localhost:8090/content/wikipedia_en_all_nopic/A/Optical_switch) – Optical switches offer faster actuation and longer life
- **Reddit**: [Not for everyone but I like it](http://localhost:9999/submission_images/e7f9024bef765bb2557de8b76cc791a0af000e47dd348bff271feebb8ea011ca.jpg) – Community embraces optical switch builds

### Myth 4: Hall-effect magnetic switches are too expensive and niche
- **Wiki**: [Hall effect sensor](http://localhost:8090/content/wikipedia_en_all_nopic/A/Hall_effect_sensor) – Hall-effect is proven industrial technology now affordable for keyboards
- **Reddit**: [Big W](http://localhost:9999/submission_images/c876b20d6b3b643774e61d6a3c0914a9bc013118c9f10bdc9ff4e940a084f4b9.jpg) – Community celebrates Hall-effect keyboard adoption

### Myth 5: More key rollover (NKRO) is essential for everyone
