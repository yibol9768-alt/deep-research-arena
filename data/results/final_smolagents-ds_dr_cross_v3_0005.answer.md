## (b) Key Community Wisdom from Reddit

Buy used office chairs from corporate liquidation sales for huge savings (from /f/LifeProTips).
Prioritize a good chair and monitor over fancy decorations (from /f/personalfinance).
Avoid buying 'ergonomic' gadgets marketed as essential; many are overpriced and unnecessary (from /f/personalfinance).
Use natural light and position your desk near a window to reduce eye strain (from /f/LifeProTips).

## (d) Buying-Priority Rank

If budget is limited, purchase items in this order:

1. **Ergonomic Office Chair**: Most critical for comfort and preventing back pain.
2. **24-inch Monitor**: Essential for productivity and reducing eye strain.
3. **Wireless Keyboard and Mouse**: Improves ergonomics and workspace flexibility.
4. **Desk Lamp**: Adequate lighting is important for reducing eye fatigue.
5. **Laptop Stand**: Improves posture but can be substituted with books initially.
6. **Air Purifier**: Health benefit but can be postponed if budget tight.
7. **Desk Organizer**: Organizational but not essential.
8. **Coffee Maker**: Convenience item; can use existing kitchen equipment.
